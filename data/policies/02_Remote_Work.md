# AddisTech Solutions — Remote Work Policy

## Purpose
To define expectations and eligibility for remote work.

## Scope
Applies to all eligible employees.

## Definitions
- Hybrid Work: Combination of remote and in-office work.

## Policy

### Eligibility
Employees must complete **90 days of employment** before becoming eligible for remote work.

### Remote Schedule
Eligible employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week**.
At least **2 days per week must be in-office**.

### Work Hours
Remote employees must be available between **9:00 AM and 4:00 PM local time**.

### Security Requirements
Employees must use:
- Company-issued devices
- VPN access
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)

### Performance Expectations
Remote work is contingent on maintaining satisfactory performance ratings.

## Exceptions
Department heads may approve temporary full-time remote arrangements.

# AddisTech Solutions — Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose
To reimburse employees for legitimate business expenses.

## Policy

### Receipt Requirement
All expenses over **$50 require an itemized receipt**.

### Submission Deadline
Expenses must be submitted within **30 days of purchase**.

### Approval
Expenses over **$500 require Director-level approval**.

### Travel Per Diem
Meal per diem is **$60 per day** when traveling overnight.

### Non-Reimbursable
- Alcohol (unless client-approved)
- Personal entertainment
- Traffic fines

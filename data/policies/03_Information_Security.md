# AddisTech Solutions — Information Security Policy

## Purpose
To protect company data and systems.

## Scope
Applies to all employees and contractors.

## Definitions
- Confidential Data: Customer, financial, or internal strategic information.
- Security Incident: Unauthorized access or data breach.

## Policy

### Password Requirements
Passwords must:
- Be at least **12 characters**
- Include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
- Be changed every **90 days**

### Multi-Factor Authentication
MFA is mandatory for:
- Email systems
- VPN access
- Financial systems

### Incident Reporting
Security incidents must be reported within **1 hour of discovery** to security@addistech.com.

### Device Usage
Only company-issued devices may access confidential systems.

### Data Storage
Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices.

## Exceptions
Temporary exceptions require written CISO approval.

## Contacts
Security Team: security@addistech.com

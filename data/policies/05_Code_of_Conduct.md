# AddisTech Solutions — Code of Conduct

## Policy

### Workplace Behavior
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are strictly prohibited.

### Reporting
Employees may report violations anonymously via the ethics hotline.

### Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose financial interests in competing businesses.

### Disciplinary Action
Violations may result in termination.

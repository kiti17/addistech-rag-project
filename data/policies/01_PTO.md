# AddisTech Solutions — Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Purpose
To provide employees with paid leave for rest, illness, and personal matters.

## Scope
Applies to all full-time and part-time employees of AddisTech Solutions.

## Definitions
- PTO: Paid Time Off combining vacation and sick leave.
- Accrual: The rate at which PTO is earned monthly.

## Policy

### Accrual
Full-time employees accrue PTO at a rate of **1.5 days per month (18 days per year)**.
Part-time employees accrue PTO proportionally based on hours worked.

### Eligibility
Employees are eligible to use PTO after **30 days of employment**.

### Carryover
Employees may carry over up to **5 unused PTO days** into the next calendar year.
Any unused PTO above 5 days will expire on December 31.

### Approval Process
PTO requests must be submitted at least **5 business days in advance** via the HR portal.
Manager approval is required.

### Sick Leave
Employees may use PTO for illness without advance notice but must notify their manager within **2 hours of shift start**.

## Exceptions
Emergency situations may waive advance notice requirements.
